Our Story

Established in the year 2000, Barista Coffee Company is the pioneer of coffee culture in India. The Barista Café chain delivers a true coffee experience in a warm, friendly, and relaxed environment. It provides a comfortable place for people to unwind over interesting conversations and a hot cup of coffee. Besides India, cafés are located across the islands of Sri Lanka and Maldives.
